What are the eligibility / minimum qualifications for joining the course?

A basic ability to read & write English or Hindi is mandatory.

You can enrol in the programme by just visiting the academy or online form and email/ send it to us. You can also call us on 8010801091.

How do I make the payment?

You can make cash payment or NEFT transfer. Course will commence upon receipt of 100% advance payment.

Is Short Term Courses available?

Yes, you have the option to do the Short Term Course, visit the Course section for more details.

We do not provide any accommodation but we can definitely recommend / assist with the names of the hostels/ estate agents for PG accommodation etc.

After the successful completion of course and giving the assigned exams the certificate will be issued.

Why are the courses so much shorter than at colleges of further education?

We provide short, intensive training courses. We work with very small numbers in each training group (maximum 8 students per tutor) to provide a more student focused learning environment.

Will I get Job assistance?

The Placement Cell is dedicated to advising students on career options and providing them with the latest information on final placement opportunities. We provide 100% job assistance.
