5. Use of cookies:

Cookies are small flies that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer’s hard drive through your web browser (if you allow) that enables the sites or service provider’s systems to recognize your browser and capture certain information.
We use cookies to help remember and process the items in your shopping cart, understand and save your preference for future visits and compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better experiences and tools in the future.
