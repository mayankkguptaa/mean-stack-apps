Crystal spa manicure

  1. Sanitize your hand first before starting the Manicure and then sanitize clientâ€™s hands.

  2. Remove all nail polish from the hands with a cotton and nail polish remover.

  3. Clip the nails. Clip it straight across.

  4. File the nails to the desired length and shape.

    • When filing nails, do not file in zig-zap but rather from the edges going up to the center of the nail.

  5. Heat Water to make crystal.

  6. Put Hand & Relaxing Soak pouch in warm water in a bowl.

  7. Massage with crystal for 5 minutes.

  8. Put de-crystalizer in remaining crystals and put this in the tub.

  9. Apply massage cream to all cuticles before you place your hands in water.

  10. Remove hands from water after 5 min.

  11. Use pusher to push back cuticles

  12. Use cuticle remover to remove cuticles

  13. Use cuticle cutter if required to cut cuticles

  14. Use exfoliating scrub to massage the hands.

  15. Use a pumic stone.

  16. Put hands back in water and clean the scrub.

  17. Now take out the next foot and repeat step 11-16.

  18. Take out 1st hand Use a manicure brush on top of the nails for a deeper clean.

  19. Use a buffer to buff on the surface of the nail for a smoother nail.

  20. Massage with massage cream.

  21. Clean hands with moist warm towel.

  22. Repeat step for next hand 18-21.

  23. Finally, the application of the nail painting. Use a base coat before applying nail colour to allow nail colour to last longer.
